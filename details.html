Festival of the collectivities, was a sucess!

2022-12-12 - Room A

The Fair Food Festival was a resounding success this year, with thousands of people coming out to enjoy delicious food, live music, and family-friendly fun. Attendees enjoyed a wide variety of food from over 100 vendors, including classic fair favorites like corn dogs, funnel cakes, and fried Oreos. There were also plenty of healthier options available, such as fresh fruit skewers, salads, and wraps.
